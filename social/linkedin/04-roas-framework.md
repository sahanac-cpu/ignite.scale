# LinkedIn post draft

The most common lie in Dubai marketing reports: ROAS.

Not because anyone's intentionally fabricating numbers. Because the standard ROAS number every agency reports is missing 5 layers of context, and brand owners almost never get shown what's actually behind it.

The 5 layers of ROAS most agencies will never show you:

1) Platform-reported ROAS — what Meta/Google says. Missing 30–45% of conversions in 2026 because iOS killed pixel tracking.

2) Server-side ROAS — actual measured conversions via Conversions API + Enhanced Conversions. Usually 15–30% higher than platform numbers.

3) Incremental ROAS — what would have happened without the ad? Brand-keyword conversions are 60–80% incremental zero. Most agencies count them anyway.

4) Cohort ROAS — first-purchase ROAS hides the truth. 12-month LTV is the real number for any brand with repeat customers.

5) Contribution margin ROAS — 4x ROAS at AED 1000 CPL with AED 400 contribution margin is a losing campaign. Almost no agency looks at your COGS.

Diagnostic questions that surface 80% of leaks in any Dubai paid account:

→ What's your Conversions API setup? (No setup = under-reporting by 25-40%)

→ How are you separating brand and non-brand search? (Mixed = algorithm cannibalises spend)

→ Show me cohort revenue by acquisition channel for the last 6 months. (Most can't produce this)

→ What's your contribution margin per SKU? (No answer = optimising for revenue, not profit)

→ When did you last run a holdout test? (Never = your incremental ROAS is whatever you want it to be)

If your agency can't answer these in under 30 minutes, your money is being managed by people who don't actually know if what they're doing works.
