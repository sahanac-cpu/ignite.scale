# The remote-agency off-page SEO playbook

Without a Google Business Profile, you lose the map-pin advantage. But you can recover most of the SEO weight by being aggressively present on platforms that DON'T require physical verification. These are the ones to do, in priority order.

## Tier 1 — do this first (high impact, easy verification)

### 1. Clutch.co — DA 87, the single most valuable agency directory

**Why:** B2B buyers literally use Clutch to research agencies before contacting them. A complete Clutch profile with 3+ reviews ranks for "Dubai marketing agency" type searches.

**Setup:**
1. Go to https://clutch.co/get-listed
2. Submit as: ignite-scale, Dubai UAE, Digital Marketing > Social Media Marketing
3. Verification: they call your business phone (+971 55 511 6465) — works for remote businesses
4. Complete every field: services, focus areas, pricing tier, team size, languages, industries served
5. Add the case studies template from `outreach/templates/04-directory-listing.md`
6. Time investment: 90 minutes setup + ongoing review collection

### 2. Bing Places for Business — Microsoft's local listing (NOT just Bing — it powers DuckDuckGo + Yahoo + ChatGPT search)

**Why:** Bing Places accepts service-area businesses without a physical visit verification. It also feeds Apple Maps and gets cited by every AI search engine (ChatGPT browse, Perplexity, Claude search).

**Setup:**
1. Go to https://www.bingplaces.com
2. Sign in with a Microsoft account (create one if needed using admin@ignite-scale.com)
3. Add business → "Service business" → no physical address required
4. Service area: Dubai + all 7 emirates
5. Verification: phone call or email (both available for service businesses)
6. Time: 20 minutes

### 3. Apple Maps Business Connect — increasingly important for iPhone-dominant UAE market

**Why:** UAE has roughly 65% iPhone share. Apple Maps doesn't require physical address verification for service businesses. iPhone Siri queries route through Apple Maps first.

**Setup:**
1. Go to https://mapsconnect.apple.com
2. Sign in with Apple ID (create dedicated business one if needed)
3. Add business → choose "Service business" type → set service area
4. Verification by phone (calls the +971 number)
5. Time: 15 minutes

### 4. LinkedIn Company Page — strong SEO signal, doubles as a credibility check for buyers

**Why:** LinkedIn pages rank well for branded searches. Buyers research agencies on LinkedIn before reaching out. Also feeds the LinkedIn auto-post agent.

**Setup:**
1. Go to https://www.linkedin.com/company/setup/new
2. Page name: ignite-scale
3. URL: linkedin.com/company/ignite-scale
4. Tagline: "Dubai growth agency engineering paid social, content and funnels for ambitious UAE brands"
5. Add logo + cover image (use og-image.jpg as cover)
6. Add the full company description from the directory template
7. Time: 25 minutes

**Once created:** add the LinkedIn URL to `sameAs` in the schema (I'll wire that up automatically once you give me the URL).

## Realistic effort + ROI

If you do Tier 1 (4 listings) in week 1 — about **2.5 hours of work** — you'll have:

- 4 backlinks from DA 70+ domains (worth ~50 low-quality backlinks each)
- 4 alternate authoritative profiles Google can cross-reference with your `sameAs` schema
- A presence on every search engine that matters in UAE (Google + Bing + Apple Maps + DuckDuckGo + ChatGPT)
- A LinkedIn page that doubles as your content amplification channel
- Eligibility for Clutch's "Top Dubai SMMA" category page once you have 3+ reviews

That's roughly **80% of what Google Business Profile would have given you**, achievable without any physical location requirement.
