**How to find broken links to replace:**

1. Pick a high-DA UAE business or marketing site
2. Use a tool like Ahrefs Site Explorer, Semrush Backlink Analytics, or the free Check My Links Chrome extension
3. Scan their outbound links for 404s
4. Find ones where you have a page that genuinely covers the same topic
5. Send this email

**Why this works:**
- You're doing them a favour first (flagging the broken link)
- Your suggestion is a natural fit, not an unsolicited pitch
- Editors update broken links proactively — very high acceptance rate

**Common targets:**
- Resource roundups on agency websites
- "Recommended reading" sections on niche marketing blogs
- Tool/service comparison pieces
