# Why multi-platform reviews matter

Google trusts a business that has consistent reviews across multiple platforms more than a business with 50 Google reviews and nothing elsewhere. The signal Google looks for is: "Is this business real enough that other sites also trust it?"

For ignite-scale, the three platforms that matter, in order:

1. **Google Business Profile** — local-search ranking, knowledge panel, direct lead generation
2. **Clutch.co** — agency-specific buyers research here; their reviews carry weight in B2B procurement
3. **Trustpilot** — broad consumer + B2B trust signal, picks up in SERPs

# Template (send to a client a few weeks after Google review, if they were positive)

Hi {Client name},

Thank you again for the kind Google review last month — it genuinely helps.

I have one more small ask. We've been listed on Clutch.co (the B2B agency directory most marketing buyers in Dubai actually research on). Their reviews are about 5-10 minutes vs Google's 1 minute, because they ask more detailed questions.

If you'd be willing to leave us one there too, the link is: {YOUR CLUTCH PROFILE URL}/leave-feedback

(They'll verify you're a real client via LinkedIn or a work email — it's a more rigorous platform, which is why their reviews carry weight.)

No worries at all if it's too much time. Either way, thanks for everything.

— Sahana

# Setup steps for Clutch (one-time)

1. Go to https://clutch.co/get-listed
2. Submit your business: ignite-scale, Dubai, Digital Marketing category
3. Verify the listing (they call you to confirm)
4. Optimise your Clutch profile: case studies, services, team page, awards
5. Save your "Leave Feedback" URL — that's what you share with clients

# Setup steps for Trustpilot (one-time)

1. Go to https://business.trustpilot.com → free tier first
2. Claim your domain ignite-scale.com
3. Verify ownership (DNS TXT record)
4. Get your "Review Invitation" link
5. Optional: pay for the Standard tier later (~$200/mo) once you have ~10 reviews — gives you automated invite emails

# Realistic timeline

Month 1: claim profiles on all three
Month 2-3: 2-3 reviews per platform (early clients)
Month 6: 8-10 reviews on Google, 3-5 on Clutch, 3-5 on Trustpilot
Month 12: 20+ on Google, 10+ on Clutch, 8+ on Trustpilot

This is what "real" looks like, and it's what Google actually ranks.
