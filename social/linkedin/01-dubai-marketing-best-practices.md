# LinkedIn post draft (text-only, 1300 char)

Most "Dubai marketing best practices" articles are written by people who have never run a campaign in the UAE.

You can tell within two paragraphs.

I run ignite-scale, a Dubai growth agency. Past two years: 50+ live campaigns across real estate, hospitality, beauty, F&B and B2B. Here are 9 things that have actually moved revenue for our clients in 2026, that almost nobody is writing about:

1. Meta has diminishing returns above AED 60k/month spend. Diversify or pay a premium for the same reach.

2. TikTok now beats Meta on CPM for under-35 Dubai audiences. The agencies still all-in on Meta are losing this quietly.

3. Bilingual (EN+AR) ads lift CTR by 18–40% in our tests. 90% of brands run English only.

4. Most reported ROAS is missing 30–45% of conversions because iOS killed client-side tracking. Conversions API is now non-negotiable.

5. Cohort LTV ROAS is the real number. First-purchase ROAS hides the brands actually winning.

6. The first 3 seconds of video creative outweigh everything else combined.

7. Landing page speed is a UAE-specific moat. 4G in Sharjah is not 5G in Downtown.

8. Click-to-WhatsApp ads produce 40–60% lower CPL than form fills in service businesses.

9. The Dubai influencer market is mismanaged. Tiered nano/micro structures beat chasing macros 3:1 on engagement-per-dollar.

The full piece, with the data and frameworks, is on our blog. Link in comments.
